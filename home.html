I am

Hello, my name is Julianne Peters.

I am currently working as an intern for a local San Diego company, The 5th Ingredient, after Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp. Through the bootcamp, an immersive style was used to teach Javascript, React, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL. While learning languages and syntax, a strong priority was placed on programmatic logic, MVC, RESTful programming, testing, and AGILE methodology in development, all while using pair programing.

My choice to transition from a 15-year career as a critical care nurse was made with an awareness of what I loved most about that work. I am passionate about spending my days committed to improving other’s lives. I thrive in the context of a fast-paced environment where I am expected to be constantly learning, problem solving, and prioritizing a complex set of demands. All of these abilities have proved key in my transition to this new and exciting field.

I’m super excited to be transferring my abilities to the tech industry because it is filled with opportunity to meet people's needs while using problem solving and logic! The bootcamp,my current internship, and dedicated personal study time has given me a solid base of information and skills upon which to draw as I look to step out of the educational environment and into the professional environment.

I’m most interested in full stack development and want to work for company that is focused on serving and meeting their needs of the client. Ideally, that company would push and support me as I continue in my professional development.
