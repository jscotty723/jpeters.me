I am

Hello, my name is Julianne Peters.

I am currently working as a remote software developer for a San Diego company, The 5th Ingredient, after having completed a Full-Stack Web Development School. I'm currently programming using the LAMP (Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP) stack. An immersive style was utilized in school to teach Javascript, React, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL. While learning languages and syntax, there was a strong prioritization on programmatic logic, MVC, RESTful programming, testing, and AGILE methodology in development, all while utilizing pair programing.

I am transitioning from a 15 year career as a critical care nurse to become a web developer, because as a nurse I love spending my days committed to improving other’s lives. This was in the context of a fast paced environment where I expected to be constantly learning, problem solving, and prioritizing a complex set of demands. All of these skills have proved very key in my transition to this new and exciting field.

I’m super excited to be transferring my skills to the tech industry because it is filled with opportunity to meet people's needs while using problem solving and logic! As I've completed school and my internship and am now working remotely, I couldn't be more excited what is ahead in this career.

I’m most interested in full stack development and want to work for company that is very focused on the client and seeking to serve and meet their needs. Personally, I want to be both pushed and supported by the company for whom I work as I continue in my professional development.
